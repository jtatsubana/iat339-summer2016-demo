Branding Ideas

  • What This Company Offers to Consumers

    FootyStats delivers free soccer stats for English Premier League teams. This will be available to the public.

  • What This Company Offers to Other Businesses

    FootyStats gives businesses the ability to purchase Ad spaces on the website.
    There are 3 tiers of ad spaces each offering different levels of flexibility and exposure.

    Type Price Service
    Gold$99/MonthAd appears on 5 Pages
    10,000 impressions guaranteed.
    Video Ad support
    Silver$69/MonthAd appears on 3 Pages
    5,000 impressions guaranteed.
    Video Ad support
    Bronze$39/MonthAd appears on 1 Page
    2,000 impressions guaranteed.

  • Who are the Potential Customers?

    FootyStats is a B2B business focused on selling ad spaces to other companies in the Soccer industry. This is because FootyStats focuses on providing stats for soccer, so naturally the audience will be interested in the sport. Therefore businesses such as Online Jersey stores, Football clubs selling their tickets, and even Sports Betting websites could be potential customers purchasing ad spaces.

  • Why might this company entice somebody to purchase the product?

    FootyStats' main audience are soccer fans. This gives advertisers a focused and narrow market to advertise on. Consequently it can save advertisers money and time because they know exactly who they are advertising for.

Branding Components

Font

A basic sans-serif font with above average amount of curviness. The primary reason for using Source Sans Pro is how approachable its numerical types are. This will come in useful when displaying large amount of statistics.

Colour Palette

  • Black : #232323 - Texts, Header, and Other Core Elements
  • Green : #135f48 - Graphical Elements, Backgrounds
  • Blue : #0066c0 - Links, etc
  • Gray : #efefef - Background, Subdued Texts

Visual Direction

FootyStats will use a subdued color palette. Images will often be overlayed with dark veils as to not "pop" too much.

This is the direction I wanted to go with because FootyStats is intended to be used as a companion to sports betting websites, which often have bright colours as part of their core color scheme.
